Tasikmalaya

Tasikmalaya in West Java offers interesting tourism with its natural beauty, culture, and hospitality of its people. Natural attractions such as Mount Galunggung and Cigamea Waterfall are breathtaking. The Weaving Craft Center and Ciburuy Site introduce its culture and history. Tasikmalaya is an authentic destination to experience local hospitality and natural beauty.

Natural Tourism

Tasikmalaya offers breathtaking natural attractions such as Situ Gede Lake, Cipelah Waterfall, and Mount Galunggung. These destinations captivate with their natural beauty, challenging hiking trails, and refreshing experiences. With its pristine natural charm, Tasikmalaya is an ideal destination for nature enthusiasts.

Cultural Tourism

Tasikmalaya offers a rich cultural tourism experience with traditional arts, Tarung Derajat martial art, festivals, historical sites, and architectural landmarks. Immerse in the local customs and traditions to appreciate the vibrant cultural scene and explore Indonesia's cultural heritage in this captivating city.

Water Park Tourism

Tasikmalaya boasts an exciting water park that promises fun-filled aquatic adventures. With thrilling water slides, lazy rivers, wave pools, and splash zones, the water park offers a refreshing escape for visitors of all ages. Whether you seek adrenaline-pumping slides or relaxation by the pool, the water park in Tasikmalaya is a perfect destination for aquatic entertainment and family-friendly enjoyment.
